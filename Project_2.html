LET ME EXPLAIN A BIT MY ROLE IN THE PROJECT

As the UX lead and researcher, I work closely with international project leads, owners, engineers, developers, and sales teams. My responsibilities include defining the overall UX strategy and goals, managing a team of UX interns and a UI designer, and conducting user research activities such as interviews and usability testing. I guide the creation of wireframes and prototypes, ensuring alignment with user experience goals and brand guidelines. I also conduct usability evaluations to propose improvements for a better user interface.

Define UX strategy and goals

Together with the project owner, we establish the production vision, collaboration goals, expectations, UX KPIs, timelines, and milestones.

Planning, staffing, budgeting

I plan PI activities and bi-weekly design sprints. I define the skills required for the UX team and recruit a part-time UI designer (consultant) and an intern researcher (for 3 months). I estimate and monitor the budget for UX activities.

Desk research and kick-off

I collected and analyzed existing research within the company and the requirement. Based on the findings, I refined detailed and context hypotheses, and list questions and doubts.  I conducted a kick-off meeting with internal subject matter experts to dress the  questions and align on the expected design outcome.

Alignment

Based on desk research findings, the project owner and I brainstorm the user workflow we aim to offer. I guide the discussion with activity diagrams. After reaching agreement, I create a service blueprint illustrating the end-to-end workflow, considering backend and multi-channel interactions. This blueprint is used to gather input from developers, requirements, and sales.

Wireframing

I use Figma to quickly design multiple wireframes, visualizing various user interactions and design options. These wireframes undergo continuous evaluation and improvement through user testing and stakeholder review.

Customer research

In collaboration with Chinese affiliates, I prepare interview toolkit to used with customer to evaluate desirability of the product and gather input on the main concerns on the technology used in the application. In person interviews were conducts by sales with 5 customers. (on going activity)

A/B testing

I conduct unmoderated and moderated A/B testing of the wireframes that are evaluated based on the UX KPIs and user input. We use the platform Usertesting.com. The results are documented in flash reports.

UI mockups

I share the wireframes with the UI designer to the creation of the Hi-Fi mockups, and supportive illustrations. I ensure the mockups respect the usability and brand guidelines.

Usability testing and role-play testing

I employ two methodologies to assess the final user experience. First, I conduct usability testing on the hi-fi mockups to evaluate user interaction and make any necessary changes before development. Second, I conduct role-play testing to evaluate the service experience, simulating interactions with multiple devices and locations to assess the "bridge moment".

The outcome of the studies support in improve the user-experience and the definition of user requirement at service levels that all devices involved should meet.

Documentation

To provide mockups to developers, the UI designer and I document user interactions and expected system outcomes in the hi-fi mockups. I ensure the Usability Engineering File is updated with each software release.

Communication and collaborations

I develop one-page slides to present the product and main workflows to stakeholders and affiliates. Every two months, I host an Info session to update all interested parties on design progress. I facilitate collaboration with various products and services to define and document user requirements at solution and service levels.
